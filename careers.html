We value our team

We are a team of ambitious, creative, analytical and technical professionals from all over the world. We have over twenty nationalities all under one roof, working towards the same goal: to produce innovative digital experiences. Every one of our team members provides invaluable insights from countries around the globe and we believe our diversity is what sets us apart.

Here are a few reasons why you'll love working at Revonic:

Full Medical Insurance

We provide full medical insurance cover to all employees which include dental, optometry and private hospital care.

Hybrid Working Model

We help our team to have a healthy work-life balance by adopting a hybrid working model. You either work from home or office. It’s up to you!

Annual Flight

For every full year of service completed, we issue you with a return economy flight to your home country.

Office Parties

We're a tight-knit family that celebrates successes, milestones, birthdays and personal achievements, together.
